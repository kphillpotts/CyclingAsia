Peace Pedalers - Peace Pedalers is a 100+ country, 50,000 mile, 5-year worldwide mountain bike expedition led by Jamie Bianchini and Garryck Hampton. What an inspiration bunch of guys. Check out their website for some cool pictures and great journals.

Jim Langley Bicycle Aficianado - Great site from an experienced bicycle mechanic. Lots of great tips for fixing your bike and riding in general. Thanks for sharing your years of experience Jim.

Myra's Bike Pages - Interesting bunch of pages from somebody very excited about cycling. Check out the Bike-Related Articles section for useful articles.

velorution - Dismantling the car economy - Cool, funny and often quirky. Nobody loves bikes more than velorution readers.

Utah Mountain Biking, Bike Repair Pages - Great index of fix-it articles. "When you've botched the repair beyond all hope, take the bike to the experts. They won't laugh." Yeah, right!
